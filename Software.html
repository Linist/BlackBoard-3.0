Microsoft Office 365 Education for students at SDU

Get Microsoft Office 365 Mac/Windows for free as a student at SDU.

  • Go to Microsoft Office 365 Education
  • Choose "Se, om du er berettiget”
  • Type your @student.sdu.dk mail address and get verified
  • Download the software to your PC or Mac or get the app for your tablet, iPad or smartphone
  • For more information, please read this quickstart guide: Office 365 Guide for students (UK) or Office 365 Guide til studerende (DK)
